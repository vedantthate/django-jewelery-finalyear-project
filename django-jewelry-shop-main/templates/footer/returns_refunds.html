Returns & Refunds

At Golden Glamour, your satisfaction is our priority. If for any reason you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Return Policy

You may return any eligible item within 10 days of delivery. To qualify for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • The item must be unused, unworn, and in its original packaging.
  • All tags, certificates, and accessories must be included.

Return shipping is free for items received damaged or if an incorrect item was delivered. For all other returns, shipping charges may apply.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will initiate the refund process. Refunds are typically processed within 7 business days to your original method of payment.

The refund process includes the following steps:

  • Inspection: Our team verifies the returned item’s condition.
  • Approval: If approved, the refund is initiated.
  • Processing: The amount is credited to your original payment method.
  • Confirmation: You’ll receive an email confirmation once the refund is processed.

Note: Refund timelines may vary depending on your payment provider or bank policies.

If you haven’t received your refund after 7 business days, we recommend contacting your bank or payment provider for further details. If the issue persists, please don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team at glamourgolden19@gmail.com.
